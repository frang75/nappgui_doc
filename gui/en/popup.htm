langrev. h1.PopUp p.PopUps are buttons that have a drop-down menu associated with them popups. Apparently they look like pushbuttons that when pressed show a list of options. In Hello PopUp and Combo! you have an example of use. li.Use popup_create to create a popup. li.Use popup_add_elem to add an item to the list. img(popups.png,0,.35).PopUps on Windows, macOS and Linux.
